---
title: 'Create Payout'
openapi: 'POST /api/v1/payouts'
---

Transfers funds from your PayGrid balance to a recipient. Returns `201` with the payout in `status: "pending"`, or `200` when replaying an existing `Idempotency-Key` (the header is optional for payouts, but recommended — see [Idempotency](/guides/idempotency)).

## Channels

| `channel` | Required fields |
| :--- | :--- |
| `mobile` | `phone` — `network` is optional (auto-detected from the number) |
| `bank` | `account` and `recipient_bank` (SWIFT code) |

`amount`, `currency` (`TZS`, `USD`, `KES`, `UGX`), and `channel` are always required. `reference` and `metadata` are optional.

## Balance and fees

The payout fee is added on top of `amount`; the response's `total_deducted` is what leaves your balance. Funds are reserved up front and automatically returned to your balance if the payout fails. A minimum payout amount applies for TZS (10,000 TZS by default; configurable per merchant).

## Errors

| HTTP | `error_code` | When |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 400 | `VALIDATION_ERROR` | Missing channel fields, unsupported channel or currency, or amount below the TZS minimum |
| 402 | `INSUFFICIENT_BALANCE` | Balance is lower than `amount` + fee |
| 403 | `TEST_MODE_UNAVAILABLE` | Test keys (`sk_test_*`) cannot create payouts |

Full list: [Errors](/guides/errors). See also the [Payouts guide](/guides/payouts).
