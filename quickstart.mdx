## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

1. A MeetPay Merchant Account.
2. Your API Key (available in your dashboard).

## Making your first request

You can check your integration by fetching your merchant profile.

<CodeGroup>
```bash cURL
curl "https://meet.briq.tz/api/v1/merchants/me" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```
</CodeGroup>
